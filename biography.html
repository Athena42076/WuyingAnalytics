My family, composed of my supportive husband, two lovely sisters, and my adorable daughter, keeps me grounded and inspires me to constantly strive for greatness. As I embark on this new professional path, I am eager to utilize my analytical skills to make a meaningful impact in the field of Data Science. I welcome collaboration and learning opportunities to further enhance my skills and contribute to innovative projects. Welcome to my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work.
